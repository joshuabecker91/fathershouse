Who We Are

We are a community of Jesus followers that believe in the Word of God and the Power of the Holy Spirit. Our desire is to see every individual come into family and experience the Love and power of God.

Mission Statement

Fathers House Spokane is a place where people from all walks of like, denominations, cultures and people groups can receive the love of God and encounter His presence. Through the Word, Worship and finding community with the Family of God.

Our Core Values

We love the Lord and love people

We believe in the authority of the written word of God

We believe in building relationship and becoming family

We gather corporately, publicly and in our homes

Your Invited

We believe in “setting the table” of an atmosphere in which all can come to receive from the Lord. Whether you need to be refreshed, healed, restored or just need community we welcome you. We believe in not just having members, but coming into family. Church should look like family where you can be yourself, but also challenged and grow in the real you whom the Lord has created. We do not want going to church to be about a service but a gathering of the saints and them becoming family!

Partner With Us

Serving: We believe that if you are coming and joining guys for weekly gatherings you are now family! We don’t want be to serve out of obligation, but when you are family you bring your part, strengths, talents and abilities. Each one has something to being and that is the beauty of the Body of Christ. 

Generosity

The Gospel is free and we are so grateful for the goodness of His grace. As grateful people, it is our joy to practice Generosity of give ing time, resourced and finances. We our 501(c)3 that must take care of our commitments and responsibilities for our corporate gatherings and resources that are needed. We believe in working together in supporting the ministry and thank you for growing in this aspect as believers. We do not believe in giving in compulsion, but each individual to partner with the Lord and give as he leads and as needs come in. We are so grateful for all that each one has given and provided as a channel of Gods provision. That is the key, He is the Provider and he gives the “seed to the sower.”
